Aletiq is a next-generation PLM platform used by industrial companies (aerospace, automotive, medical devices) to centralize their technical data and processes.

- Designed and built an AI assistant integrated directly into the PLM, using RAG to let users query technical documents (ISO standards, assembly drawings, industrial specs) in natural language
- Built the document indexer responsible for ingesting and processing thousands of customer PDFs at scale into the RAG system
- Implemented AI-powered suggestion features for related documents and products, improving navigation and traceability across the PLM
- Full-stack development of core PLM features in TypeScript (Effect.ts, React) and Haskell
- Ongoing research and evaluation of LLM capabilities to identify and integrate relevant AI features into the product
